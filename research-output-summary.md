## Generation-Specific Highlights

### C3 (1968-1982)
- Analog systems: points ignition, vacuum-operated headlights, manual steering
- Key mods: HEI distributor conversion, Borgeson power steering box, electric headlight actuators
- MSD ignition boxes, Pertronix Ignitor modules, Weber/Holley carb swaps
- Leaf spring rear (unique to C3)

### C4 (1984-1996)
- TPI → LT1 transition mid-generation
- Digital dash cluster bezels (aftermarket LCD replacements)
- Bilstein/Koni shock upgrades, polyurethane bushing kits
- Column lock bypass modules

### C5 (1997-2004)
- LS1 architecture — first LS gen, huge aftermarket
- Column Lock Module (LMC5) bypass, T56 short throw shifters
- DeWitts radiator + SPAL fan upgrades
- Corvette-style fuel filter/regulator for LS swaps

### C6 (2005-2013)
- LS2/LS3/LS7 — most complete aftermarket ecosystem
- Cam packages (228/232 profiles), ported intake manifolds
- American Racing Headers long tube systems
- Gen III/IV LS cross-compatibility with C5

### C7 (2014-2019)
- LT1/LT4 architecture, direct injection
- Soler/DLX throttle controllers, BlendMount radar mounts
- WEAPON-X cooling kits for Z06/LT4
- Carbon fiber intake systems (aFe), ATAK/SwitchFire exhausts
- NPP valve simulators for non-valved exhaust swaps

### C8 (2020-present)
- Mid-engine paradigm — completely new mod categories
- **Unique C8 parts**: Jacking pucks, grill mesh guards, heat blankets for cats
- **DCT-specific**: Deep sump pans, billet filter covers, clutch pressure modules, magnetic shift paddles
- **HTC vs Coupe**: Clear polycarbonate engine covers for HTC
- Front lift lowering collars, Z51 coilover spanner wrenches
- MagRide compatibility filtering critical

## Cross-Generational Compatibility Notes
- LS Gen III/IV valvetrain (trunnion kits, rocker arms) fits C5 + C6 + LS-swapped C3/C4
- Brembo/Wilwood brake consumables often cross C6/C7/C8
- Fire extinguisher mounts, harness bars, 5-point harnesses cross C5/C6/C7
- Wheels: bolt patterns changed across gens — MUST enforce per-gen fitment
